Threats

You are given a position and a move, the challenge is to find the best move for your opponent. This will learn you to think for your opponent and train to see the board from the other side.

Example 1

When black plays g5, find the best move for white
Solution is Bg3.


Example 2

When black plays Qe1, find the best move for white
Solution is Kc2.
