Opening

The opening module is an extra module. This module costs 10 euro! The opening module helps you to train a general opening repertoire. You can play either white or black, and realchess will play the other site. Realchess will choose moves according to the opening repertoire of grandmasters. The opening module has three options:
  • Train your black repertoire
  • Train your white repertoire
  • Repeat the previously learned openingmoves according to the principle of spaced repetition
