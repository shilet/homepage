Resume

 Sheila Timp was born on March 29 1973 in The Hague, the Netherlands. After completing grammar school she entered medical school in 1991 at the University of Utrecht and obtained her medical degree in 1999. In the same year she graduated in Psychosocial Sciences at the SPSO Utrecht.
In 2000, she joined the chair of Signals and Systems of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Twente, Enschede to work on a project for real-time visualisation of three dimensional medical data. There she also obtained her propedeutic degree in mathematics.
September 2001 she started a Ph.D. project under supervision of Nico Karssemeijer at the Radiology Department of the University Medical Centre St. Radboud Nijmegen. Her research focused on the use of temporal information to improve computer aided detection and diagnosis programmes for mammography. After the successful defence of her thesis in 2006 she started as researcher at the Radiology department, University Medical Centre Groningen.
At the moment she works at Xpar Vision on the development of an inspection and control system for the container glass industry.
